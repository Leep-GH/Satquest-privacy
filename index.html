This Privacy Policy explains how our educational app handles information when you use our service. ## Information We Collect **SATs Quest does NOT collect, store, or transmit any personal information.** Our app is designed with privacy in mind: - ✅ **No personal data collection** - We don't ask for names, emails, or any personal details - ✅ **No internet connection required** - All content is stored locally on your device - ✅ **No user accounts** - No registration or login required - ✅ **No tracking** - We don't track your usage or behavior - ✅ **No third-party analytics** - No external services monitor your activity ## Local Data Storage SATs Quest stores the following information **locally on your device only**: - Quiz progress and scores within each session - App settings and preferences (question count, etc.) This data: - Never leaves your device - Is not transmitted to any servers - Can be deleted by uninstalling the app - Is not accessible to us or any third parties ## Children's Privacy SATs Quest is designed for children aged 10-11 (Year 6 students). All app functionality operates entirely offline on your device. ## Changes to This Policy If we make any changes to this Privacy Policy, we will update the effective date above and notify users through app store updates. ## Contact Information If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us: **Email:** [satquest.help@gmail.com] **Developer:** [Quest Apps] ## Your Rights Since we don't collect personal data, there is no personal information to access, correct, or delete.
